# Profiles

The Profiles page lets you discover and follow traders on Cronos.

## Viewing a Profile

Every wallet address on cro.trade has a profile page. You can view a trader's profile by:

- Tapping a trader's address in the trade feed
- Searching for a wallet address or .cro name
- Navigating to the Profiles tab

A profile shows:

- **Wallet address** and .cro name (if registered)
- **Avatar** — auto-generated or custom
- **Follow/Unfollow** button
- **Follower and following counts**
- **Trade history** — recent trades made by this wallet
- **Holdings** — tokens currently held by the wallet

## Following Traders

Tap the **Follow** button on any profile to add them to your followed list.

- Your followed traders appear on the Profiles page for quick access
- Following is tied to your connected wallet
- You can unfollow at any time by tapping the button again

## .cro Names

Traders who have registered a Cronos ID (`.cro` name) will display their human-readable name instead of a raw wallet address. For example, `alice.cro` instead of `0x1234...abcd`.

## Discovering Traders

Browse the Profiles page to find active traders. You can also discover traders organically by tapping addresses in trade feeds across the app.
